# Cover Letter Positioning

I developed AutoDoc AI Pipeline, an AI-powered vehicle document intelligence project, and expanded it into an LLM infrastructure simulation. The system is designed around containerized FastAPI services, worker queues, extraction workflows, observability dashboards, Kubernetes-style deployment manifests, and CI/security checks. This helped me understand not only how AI features are built, but how AI workloads can be deployed, monitored, scaled, and operated reliably.
